Our AI Readiness Assessment Service

We analyze your business across four key pillars:

1.  Strategy & Goals:
    - Do you have a clear business problem for AI to solve?
    - Is there executive buy-in for an AI initiative?

2.  Data Readiness:
    - Where is your data? Is it centralized?
    - Is data quality high and is it accessible?
    - Do you have data governance policies in place?

3.  Technology & Infrastructure:
    - Are you on the cloud? The cloud is the foundation for AI.
    - Can your current infra support data pipelines and model training?
    - We recommend AWS services like S3, Redshift, and SageMaker.

4.  People & Skills:
    - Do you have data scientists or analysts?
    - Is the team trained on data-driven decision making?
